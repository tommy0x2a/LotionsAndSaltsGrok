How it all began

From My Kitchen
to Your Tub

👩‍🍳

Hi, I'm Elena.

In 2022, I was going through a really stressful chapter of my life. My skin was reacting badly to everything — commercial lotions, fancy serums — nothing helped. My grandmother used to make simple salves from herbs in her garden, so I started experimenting.

What began as a small pot of shea butter and lavender essential oil on my stove turned into something I couldn't stop sharing with friends and family. They kept asking for more. Their skin calmed down. They slept better. They felt taken care of.

Willow & Sage Co. was born from that belief: that self-care doesn't need to be complicated, expensive, or full of ingredients you can't pronounce. It just needs to be honest, gentle, and made with love.

— Elena Voss, Founder

The Process

1

Source the finest ingredients

Every ingredient is hand-selected from trusted, ethical suppliers. Himalayan salts from Pakistan, shea butter from Ghana, essential oils from family farms.

2

Mix in small batches

No giant vats or automated machines. Every batch is blended by hand in my kitchen using carefully calibrated measurements and the same recipe I refined over 18 months.

3

Rest & quality check

Lotions need 24 hours to set. Bath salts are checked for scent balance. Nothing leaves my kitchen unless it meets my standards — which are high.

4

Pack with love & ship fast

Each order is packed by hand, wrapped in recycled tissue paper, and includes a personal thank-you card. Ships within 2 business days.
