CHAPTER I

From my kitchen
to your tub

Hi, I’m Elena Voss. In 2022, after years of battling stress-induced eczema and sensitive skin, I started mixing small batches of lotion and bath salts in my tiny Portland kitchen using ingredients I could trust.

What began as a personal healing ritual quickly became something bigger. Friends started asking for jars. Then friends of friends. Today, Willow & Sage is still made by hand in that same kitchen — but now shared with thousands of people who believe self-care should feel intentional, warm, and deeply personal.

— Elena Voss, Founder
Portland, Oregon
Photo by Elena’s sister, 2024
